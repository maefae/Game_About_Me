Educational Background

My name is Megan Seibert-Hughes and I was born in Laredo Texas. I spent a good portion of my childhood traveling with my family, but eventually I found myself living in Austin Texas by the age of 17. I went to Austin Community College for a few years before ultimately joining the military by age 20. Upon graduation of basic training, I transferred straight into UT Austin as Psychology major. Unfortunately, the pandemic hit half way into my semester and I did not want to take college courses remotely, so I got the opportunity to work as a medical courier. I also started my own pet sitting business whilst balancing the military. I'm tired of balancing three careers so that's why I decided to join Code Fellows to hopefully gain the knowledge and experience necessary to get a foot in the door of the tech industry.
